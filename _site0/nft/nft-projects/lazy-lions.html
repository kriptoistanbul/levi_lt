    Lazy Lions NFT: how big will it become?

    It is no secret that the non-fungible token (NFT) craze has taken the crypto world by storm in 2021. A project such as Lazy Lions NFT is expected to become one of the biggest players in the NFT industry. As a result of its progress over the past 30 days, the project is on track to become the undisputed third largest project in this field after CryptoPunks and Bored Apes Yacht Club.

    A unique collection of one-of-a-kind lions, Lazy Lions was launched during a lockdown in Sydney, Australia, and quickly sold out after the launch. A large online community supports the project, with each non-fungible token available for purchase and sale on the OpenSea online marketplace.

    The lowest price of the Lazy Lions NFT was $2,330 after a month of trading on the market. Compared to Bored Apes Yacht Club, which had a low price of $965 at an appropriate point in its growth. This price currently has a reserve price of approximately $1.137,78, as of July 17th 2022, this price is quite competitive.

    It is worth noting that the Lazy Lions project maintains its comparison with similar projects, despite the fact that non-fungible tokens have been recorded across the board over the past few weeks. There is no doubt that other top projects have already traded desirable NFTs for millions of dollars.

    Why has the growth been so rapid?

    A strong wallet distribution is a key component that can ensure the success of the project. Over 4,000 inpiduals claim ownership of the Lazy Lions. In the future, further growth will be indicated by this distribution.

    Additionally, most owners are participating in the NFT sector for the first time. Lazy Lions Founder Ashur said:

    “It's an exciting time to enter the NFT space; We firmly believe that this is just the beginning of a major development that will impact society as a whole. We have a lot of respect for the existing players in the area like the Bored Apes and CryptoPunks. However, that third place is really up for grabs and we're putting a lot of effort into making this place our own.

    “In our first month, these efforts have focused on building our community, particularly through Discord and Twitter. For us, the project is the community and the community is the project.”

    Lion's designs are relatable and clean, which is why owners trust them right away. A total of 160 unique characteristics were used to create each of these digital animals. These characteristics include the mane, clothes, expressions, and much more.

    For all Lazy Lions, 80% of new NFTs are free. According to the developers, all owners will receive an NFT banner image showing their unique random generated private bungalow. These bungalows are open and each Lazy Lion gets one. A Lazy Lions game will be revealed at the end with community prizes.

    In addition to Roarwards, your LL provides you with a number of other utilities.
