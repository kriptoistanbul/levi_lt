    What is IOTA?

    In the world of cryptocurrencies, IOTA is known for its innovative creation methods as a result of its decentralization. This is the most popular German cryptocurrency, created in Berlin.

    As a means of payment, this currency can be used indefinitely. Furthermore, it provides unparalleled transaction speeds and eliminates the need for long waiting periods.

    In the German and international money markets, the creation of this currency was an innovation. It is an electronic currency that has very good prospects and has had a good price performance since its introduction.

    IOTA: Price ι Price Development

    Since the launch of this cryptocurrency, several milestones have already shaped its history:

    • June 2017: IOTA can be purchased on the first exchange, Bitfinex, with a starting price of €0.64.
    • Autumn 2017: Bosch and Volkswagen are the first companies to participate in this cryptocurrency. This brings the starting price to 0.80 €.
    • December 19, 2017: The price of IOTA rises to €4.81.
    • April 2018: The first IOTA charging station is inaugurated in the Netherlands. The price rises again to about 2.00 €.
    • December 2018: The IOTA price settles between €0.19 and €0.33.
    • May 2019: Coordicie is implemented and strengthens trust in the cryptocurrency. Its price is €0.40.
    • Autumn 2019 and spring 2020: The price of IOTA remains unchanged at €0.25.
    • March 13, 2020: IOTA reaches a low of €0.0715.
    • Summer 2020: The price stabilizes again at € 0.25.
    • Spring 2021: As part of the general bull market, the IOTA rate rises to €2.17 by mid-April.
    • Summer 2021: After the price correction in May, the price settles at € 0.60.
