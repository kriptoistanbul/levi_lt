    How does ApeCoin work?

    ApeCoin operates according to a governance model, which means that the more shares you own in ApeCoin, the more you have the right to vote on future decisions related to APE. In view of the short history of ApeCoin, there are currently few examples of its application, however it is certain that the first votes relating to its strategic orientation will take place in the future.

    Additionally, ApeCoin currently offers only a limited number of specific features. Especially at the beginning, speculation plays an important role, but in the future concrete functions and use cases will take precedence. ApeCoin is a cryptocurrency that is based on the Ethereum blockchain. It is also used to create many other cryptocurrencies within the ERC-20 framework. 

    In addition, it is important to note that a significant portion of the tokens issued during the ICO were awarded to the owners of the Bored Ape Yacht Club NFT project. NFT owners received approximately 10,000 APE tokens - at today's exchange rate of approximately 13 US dollars, this is equivalent to approximately 130,000 US dollars - roughly half of what a Bored Ape NFT costs today.

    Is Apecoin a good investment

    Is Apecoin a good investment, Despite their extreme pain, bear markets often provide investors with meaningful investment opportunities. There is perhaps no better example of a sensible investment opportunity than the ApeCoin (APE), the token of the Bored Ape Yacht Club (BAYC). A token based on the NFT model was launched by Yuga Labs in March, quickly becoming one of the largest cryptocurrencies related to NFTs in the world.

    Over the last two months, ApeCoin has suffered a shocking 90 percent decline (from its all-time high). Otherside, Yuga Labs' largest project to date, has greatly influenced the development of the token. Users can use their NFTs as avatars in this extensive metaverse role-playing game. The APE token provides the backbone of Otherside and serves as both a currency and a governance token.

    There are favorable conditions for the Otherside game and the APE. To make this a reality, Yuga is working closely with Animoca brands, the current king of the metaverse. In addition to the strong duo behind the token, it has more potential than nearly any other alternative currency due to its artificially depressed price.

    What will Apecoin be worth? 

    ApeCoin may be a particularly attractive investment option for long-term investors with a strong belief in the success of Yuga Labs and the Metaverse genre in general. APE's preliminary reserve price is difficult to predict. It is possible that soil formation has already been completed. There is a possibility that APE will continue to fall for a few more months before it begins to rise again. There has been a significant amount of volatility in the APE market, making it even more difficult to predict the future direction of the market.

    There have been highs of about $27.50 and lows of about $3.20 in the few months this stock has been trading. The metaverse trend continues to flourish, and Yuga Labs and Animoca Brands are developing a great metaverse game, it is difficult to believe that APE will not reach new all-time highs in the future. In the long run, if both of these conditions hold true, then APE could turn out to be the most profitable altcoin investment of the year.

    Will ApeCoin rise?

    Will ApeCoin rise? In the near future, ApeCoin may be able to reach its all-time high, but this will not be so easy as it is with many other altcoins. It would appear that the high of approximately $27.50 (depending on the stock market) is not entirely natural. Due to the imprint Otherside of the NFT, the price was greatly inflated. Rather than using Ethereum to handle the expensive sale, Yuga Labs chose to use ApeCoin. Due to a large amount of APE being purchased to participate in the sale, the price of the APE increased significantly.

    In addition, the sale resulted in an artificial drop in price following the conclusion of the sale. There were many prospects who had already purchased APEs but did not have sufficient funds to pay the high transaction fees (more than $6,000, not including the cost of the coin). There were many who were unable to do anything else with the token. Therefore, they decided to sell it for whatever price they could find, causing the price to significantly decline. Yuga Labs has carefully planned the future of the token in order to ensure its success. Choosing to sell in ApeCoin rather than in Ethereum seemed to be a completely irrational decision.

    The total number of Otherside NFTs sold by Yuga was approximately 55,000. Yuga demanded 305 APE per NFT in public sales. Miners who possessed a Bored Ape or a Mutant Ape were able to receive their APE through the free airdrop. In the days leading up to the sale, most people bought them on the open market for more than $20. For a price of $20, the cost of each NFT would be more than $6,000, more than two Ethereum at the time. Approximately 75 million ApeCoins were generated through the sale. Instead of charging 1 ETH per NFT, the company could charge 2 ETH instead. The decision only makes sense if comprehensive plans have already been developed for the future support of APE. APE will likely be much more than merely a governance token, though it is difficult to determine what exactly that means.

    Does Apecoin have potential in the long run?

    ApeCoin is clearly not as secure an investment over the long term as Ethereum and Bitcoin, which are more established cryptocurrencies. Even so, its relatively small size makes it more likely to become a "moonshot." It is likely that ApeCoin is a valuable investment for the long term in the metaverse. The company has several significant advantages over its competitors. Yuga Labs is one of the largest start-up companies in the field of NFTs and Metaverse. APE appears to be very committed to helping Otherside succeed, and it appears to have the resources to do so.
