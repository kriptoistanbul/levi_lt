    What is EOS

    EOS is the native cryptocurrency of EOS.IO Blockchain. EOS.IO is a leading open source platform that focuses mainly on blockchain innovation, development and performance. EOS. IO is used by developers and entrepreneurs around the world who depend on the creation of effective, transparent and secure digital infrastructures to make their projects successful.

    EOS was launched in June 2018 by Dan Larimer and Brendan Bloomer. It was founded by Block.one and launched by Block.one. In June 2017, EOS tokens were first offered to the public. Among his accomplishments, Lamimer founded Bitshares and Steemit prior to the creation of EOS.
