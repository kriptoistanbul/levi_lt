    Global regulations for buying crypto with Paypal

    KYC refers to a system for verifying identity. In English, it refers to regulations for preventing money laundering in financial transactions. The term means know your customer in English. In addition to cryptocurrency brokers, PayPal also uses this identification method.

    PayPal is required to comply with Anti-Money Laundering (AML) regulations as an authorized financial services provider. It is mandatory to submit official photo identification, such as a passport or driver's license, as part of the protocol for proving the identity of each customer. Address verification is also frequently required.

    Pros and cons: Buy cryptocurrencies with PayPal

    PayPal is an extremely convenient method of purchasing cryptocurrencies; however, as with any form of payment, it also has advantages and disadvantages. In this way, some customers prefer credit cards while others find their fees excessive. The cheapest way to trade cryptocurrencies is to use cash transfers, but they can be quite slow and complicated. The following are the pros and cons of using PayPal when purchasing cryptocurrencies:

    Advantages

    • Convenience: All your transaction details are stored in your PayPal account. You do not have to reenter all the information if you often transact with the same broker.
    • Transaction costs: Although it depends on the conditions of your bank operator, bank transfers are usually the cheapest payment method. PayPal, however, has significantly lower transaction costs and fees than credit cards.
    • Security: PayPal works with the latest data encryption and software. In order to prevent theft and fraud, the platform is constantly updated and is very secure.
    • Buyer Protection: PayPal allows you to cancel unsatisfactory transactions. Your right to cancel the transaction is available if you do not receive the product, in this case, the cryptocurrency. It is important to note, however, that this warranty is subject to certain terms and limitations.
    • Rewards: PayPal offers rewards to users who link a credit card to their account. Make sure that you are familiar with your account terms and that you claim your purchase rewards in accordance with those terms.
    • Ease of use: PayPal's platform has a user-friendly and intuitive interface. Customer service is good and the website is easy to navigate.
    • Possibility of anonymity: Although we do not recommend it, it is theoretically possible to hide your identity with PayPal. There is the possibility of using virtual credit cards and email accounts that have been set up specifically for this purpose. It may not be necessary to conduct an identity check depending on the amount of the transaction.

    Disadvantages

    • Certain level of vulnerability: Despite best efforts to ensure security, PayPal is a target for hackers. Cyber criminals are very attracted to it due to its popularity as a payment platform. Therefore, there is a potential risk.
    • Device security: In addition to the protections implemented by the platform, you should ensure the security of your own devices. A lost or stolen computer or smartphone may be able to access your PayPal account if you store your password on it.
